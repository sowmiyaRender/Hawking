History

Mysuru Hawking embarked on its journey in 2022 as an online grocery store, aiming to provide a one-stop solution for all household needs. Our commitment to quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction quickly earned us a reputation as a reliable and trusted provider in the community.
Recognizing the growing demand for efficient and sustainable logistics, we pivoted in 2023 to become a dedicated logistics company. Partnering with Dmart, we began offering electric vehicle (EV) delivery services, combining our expertise in logistics with a commitment to environmental sustainability. Our fleet of EVs, operated by skilled drivers, ensures timely, reliable, and eco-friendly deliveries.
This transformation was driven by our vision to provide superior logistics solutions for multiple online orders, reflecting our dedication to innovation and sustainability. Today, Mysuru Hawking stands as a forward-thinking logistics company, continually evolving to meet the needs of our partners and the community, while promoting a greener future.

Mission

Our Mission

At Mysuru Hawking, our mission is to revolutionize urban logistics through sustainable and efficient delivery solutions. We are committed to providing exceptional logistics services to our partners, like Dmart, by leveraging cutting-edge electric vehicle technology and skilled drivers. Our goal is to ensure timely, reliable, and eco-friendly deliveries that contribute to a greener future while meeting the highest standards of customer satisfaction.

Vission

Our Vission

Our vision is to be the leading provider of sustainable logistics solutions in urban areas, setting the benchmark for innovation, efficiency, and environmental responsibility. We aim to expand our network, enhance our technological capabilities, and continuously improve our services to support the growing demands of the retail industry. Through our dedication to excellence and sustainability, we envision a future where every delivery is a step towards a cleaner, more connected world.
